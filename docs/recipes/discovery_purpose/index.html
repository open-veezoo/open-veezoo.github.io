Veezoo Discovery: Increase Adoption of Self-Service Analytics Through Guided Exploration

Natural language analytics solutions like Veezoo can transform the way businesses access and understand their data.

However, when implementing a self-service analytics option, companies may be concerned about potential adoption issues. Users might struggle with formulating the right questions, ask the wrong questions, or simply not know where to start in order to get the most valuable insights.

Veezoo Discovery: A Guided Pathway to Data Insights​

Veezoo Discovery was designed to address the challenges faced by users when they begin using natural language analytics solutions. Its primary goal is to provide users with a sense of accomplishment right from the start. By offering a guided and intuitive experience, Veezoo Discovery effectively boosts user confidence, ensuring a successful and seamless adoption of the self-service analytics solution.

As an integral part of the Veezoo platform, Discovery is easily accessible within the Chat UI. Users can quickly access the predefined questions and categories based on their needs, bringing any relevant discoveries into their chat history for further examination.

Purpose & Value Proposition of Veezoo Discovery​

  1. Reduces blank page syndrome: Discovery (additionally to Veezoo Boards, Auto Complete & Question Pills) helps new users overcome the intimidation of a blank page by presenting them with predefined questions and insights to click around and explore, giving them a starting point for their data exploration.

  2. Encourages user adoption: Ensuring a success feeling for first-time users is crucial to increase the adoption of the solution within an organization. Providing a guided pathway significantly enhances the user experience and encourages them to use the platform successfully & more often.

  3. Educates users within the platform: Veezoo Discovery helps educate users about the various possible use-cases and scenarios where Veezoo can be valuable. By showcasing specific questions and visualizations that can be answered using the platform, users can better understand the breadth and depth of Veezoo's capabilities.

  4. Reveals collective interests: The system allows users to see what other people in the organization are interested in, fostering a sense of collaboration and prioritizing shared business goals.

  5. Reduces dashboard complexity: Instead of creating multiple, often overlapping or redundant, dashboards, Discovery serves as a centralized hub where users can consume interesting insights and avoid dashboard clutter.

  6. Demonstrates examples: Veezoo Discovery displays concrete examples of how users can ask questions and what the resulting visualizations would look like, empowering users to model their own inquiries based on the examples provided.

  7. Serves as a starting point: Users are not limited to the answer they see in Discovery, but can actively follow-up on the received answers.
