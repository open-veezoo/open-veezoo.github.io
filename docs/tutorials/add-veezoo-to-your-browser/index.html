  • Add Veezoo to your browser

How to add Veezoo to your browser as a search engine?

1. Open your browser settings page (e.g. in Chrome)​

2. Go to "Search engine"​

3. Click on "Manage search engines"​

4. Add a Search engine​

You will have to provide:

  • a name for your search engine, e.g. Veezoo
  • a shortcut, e.g. vz
  • the URL: https://app.veezoo.com/ask/%s?id=YOUR-KG-ID-HERE
    • don't forget to substitute YOUR-KG-ID-HERE with the id you see on the URL when you open Veezoo with the Knowledge Graph you want to search.

5. Now type the keyword in your browser​

6. Ask the question​

7. Get the answer!​
